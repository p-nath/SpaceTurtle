  • 1. Preparation

    15 minutes

    For all of the exercises we will be using Trinket. It is an online platform that is used to learn as well as teach python programs.

    • Click and open the links given for each activity in the browser.(seperate links are given under each activity name)
    • Maximize the size of the screen by clicking on the ‘ ≡ ’ (triple bar) button on the top left corner and select the Fullscreen option.
    • Then move your cursor to the horizontal bar just above the Enter Command section that will turn the mouse to a double headed arrow ' ↕ '. Drag the mouse downwards to increase the size of the window for better viewability.
    • To know the instructions of the activity, click on the Instruction tab on the right side of the screen.
    • To enter the commands, type them in the window below where it says Enter Command:.Press enter on keyboard to type in the next command.
    • The Result area shows the visuals of the activity as well as your progress.
    • You can see behind the scenes(i.e, the code) by clicking on the pen button at the top bar.
